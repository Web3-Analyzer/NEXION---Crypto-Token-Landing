The Problem & Solution

DeFi infrastructure is fragmented, expensive, and captured by insiders.

The Problem

Today's on-chain protocols extract fees without returning value to participants. Governance is theater — a handful of whale wallets control outcomes. Builders pay platform taxes they never agreed to, and liquidity is siloed across chains with no unified incentive layer.

The Solution

NEXION creates a single utility layer where fee revenue is redistributed to $NXN stakers in real-time, governance is weighted by participation (not just holdings), and builders deploy cross-chain with zero protocol tax.
